    I started this awhile ago...

    Hello. My name is John Eric Arterberry. Back in 2016, I created a blog called Lean Tester. It went nowhere. But I kept at it. Then I decided in 2021, to start migrating this to some place better and I changed the name to something a bit controversal -- and here we are.

    Bottomline, I just wanted a place to share my thoughts on QA. It's what I've been doing for many years, wearing different hats, for a variety of companies and I really love this stuff!

    So that's that. Enjoy.
